Getting Here

Travel Information

By Air

The nearest airport is Shaheed Bhagat Singh International Airport (IXC) in Chandigarh, approximately 70 km from Ramada Kasauli. The drive takes approximately 2 hours. Cabs can be booked directly from the airport, and Ubers are available as well.

By Train

The nearest railway station is Chandigarh Junction (CDG), approximately 50 km from Ramada Kasauli. The drive takes around 1.5 hours. Taxis and Ubers can be easily arranged from the station.

By Road

From Indira Gandhi International Airport Terminal 3 (DEL), Ramada Kasauli is approximately 325 km, with an estimated travel time of 6 hours. Pre-booked cabs as well as Ubers are convenient options for this route.
